1. Cell

The cell is the basic structural and functional unit of life. All living organisms are composed of one or more cells that carry out essential life processes.

2. Discovery of Cell

The cell was first discovered by Robert Hooke in 1665 while examining a thin slice of cork under a microscope.

3. Types of Cells

  • Prokaryotic cells
  • Eukaryotic cells
  • Plant cells
  • Animal cells
