1. Plant Tissues

Plant tissues are groups of cells with similar structure and function. They are classified into meristematic and permanent tissues.

2. Meristematic Tissues

Meristematic tissues are actively dividing tissues responsible for the growth of plants. They are found in root tips, shoot tips, and cambium.

  • Apical meristem
  • Intercalary meristem
  • Lateral meristem

3. Permanent Tissues

Permanent tissues are derived from meristematic tissues and have lost the ability to divide. They perform specialized functions.

  • Simple tissues ‚Äì parenchyma, collenchyma, sclerenchyma
  • Complex tissues ‚Äì xylem and phloem

4. Plant Morphology

Plant morphology deals with the external structure and form of different parts of plants such as roots, stems, leaves, flowers, fruits, and seeds.

5. Functions of Root, Stem & Leaf

  • Root ‚Äì absorption and anchorage
  • Stem ‚Äì support and transport
  • Leaf ‚Äì photosynthesis and transpiration
