1. Introduction to Geometry

Geometry is the branch of mathematics that deals with shapes, sizes, positions, angles, and dimensions of objects. It plays an important role in measurement and spatial understanding.

2. Plane Geometry

Plane geometry deals with two-dimensional figures such as lines, angles, triangles, quadrilaterals, circles, and polygons.

3. Areas of Plane Figures

  • Area of rectangle and square
  • Area of triangle
  • Area of circle
  • Area of parallelogram and trapezium

4. Solid Geometry

Solid geometry involves three-dimensional figures such as cubes, cuboids, cylinders, cones, and spheres.

5. Volumes and Surface Areas

  • Volume of cube and cuboid
  • Volume of cylinder, cone, and sphere
  • Surface area calculations

6. Applications of Geometry in Pharmacy

  • Calculation of container volumes
  • Measurement of dosage forms
  • Design of pharmaceutical equipment
