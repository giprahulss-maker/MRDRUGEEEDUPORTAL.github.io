1. Reading Skills

Reading skills involve the ability to understand, interpret, and evaluate written information effectively. Good reading skills are essential for academic success and lifelong learning.

2. Types of Reading

  • Skimming
  • Scanning
  • Intensive reading
  • Extensive reading

3. Listening Skills

Listening is an active process that requires concentration and understanding of spoken messages. Effective listening improves comprehension and interpersonal communication.

4. Writing Skills

Writing skills include the ability to express ideas clearly and logically in written form. It involves proper grammar, vocabulary, organization, and clarity.

5. Importance of Reading, Listening & Writing Skills

  • Enhances academic performance
  • Improves professional communication
  • Builds critical thinking ability
  • Supports lifelong learning
