1. Pharmaceutical Solutions

Solutions are liquid dosage forms in which one or more substances are completely dissolved in a suitable solvent or mixture of solvents.

2. Classification of Solutions

  • Oral solutions
  • Topical solutions
  • Ophthalmic solutions
  • Parenteral solutions

3. Advantages and Disadvantages

Solutions provide uniform drug distribution and rapid absorption but may have stability issues and require preservatives.

4. Factors Affecting Solubility

  • Nature of solute and solvent
  • Temperature
  • pH of the medium
  • Use of co-solvents

5. Pharmaceutical Applications

Solutions are widely used for oral, topical, ophthalmic, and injectable drug delivery due to ease of administration and rapid onset of action.
