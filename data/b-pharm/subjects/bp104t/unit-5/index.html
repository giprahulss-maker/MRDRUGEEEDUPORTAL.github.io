1. Dental Products

Dental products are inorganic pharmaceutical preparations used for oral hygiene and prevention of dental disorders such as caries and plaque formation.

2. Dental Abrasives

  • Calcium carbonate
  • Dicalcium phosphate
  • Calcium pyrophosphate
  • Hydrated silica

3. Anti-Caries Agents

Fluorides such as sodium fluoride and stannous fluoride strengthen tooth enamel and prevent dental caries by increasing resistance to acid attack.

4. Radiopharmaceuticals

Radiopharmaceuticals are radioactive compounds used for diagnosis and therapy. They emit radiation that helps in imaging or treatment of specific organs.

5. Applications of Radiopharmaceuticals

  • Diagnostic imaging (PET, SPECT)
  • Thyroid disorders treatment
  • Cancer diagnosis and therapy
